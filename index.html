What is the Auranous Portal ?

It is a decentralized application that connects NFT projects with potential buyers adding a new experience to the new web, and constantly changing through a governance Dao managed by contributors.

FOR CONTRIBUTORS

Contributors decide which projects get a place in the portal and which don't, how the portal takes the path in development, and what utilities to add/remove.

About

Auranous Portal is a web 3.0 gateway for newcomers. Simply enter the portal and you will be teleported to a random NFT collection page. A perfect tool to find fresh projects. The projects go through multiple security tests to verify, showing you fresh and secured projects. Use Auranous Portal to navigate NFT projects on the decentralized web.
